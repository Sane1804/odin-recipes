Tuna-salad ingredients

  • Tuna tin
  • Onion
  • Tomatos
  • Avocados
  • Corn tin
  • salt and pepper
  • mayonnaise

Preparation

  1. cut the onion, tomato and avocado in Brunoise
  2. add the onion, tomatos, avocados and corn tin in a bowl add some salt and mix
  3. The add the tuna to the bowl, add the mayonnaise
  4. add salt and pepper and mix it again
